About Marcopolo Events

MarcoPolo Ongoing success in serving a wide client base offering décor for weddings, Corporate events; from personal functions to exhibitions and everything in between Providing a total managed service for any event, has truly made mark within the industry. Recognized & respected for our quality of work & exceptional customer care,

we pride Ourselves in offering a personal service to develop innovative creation for our clients.With our extensive portfolio, we offer a wide range of services to cater for any event Including A bespoke design & event management service where we can create a unique event…Just for you !

We have proudly served over 20,000 guests, managed over 100 events and served long list of corporate clients.

Features

  1. Event Management: Marcopolo Events provides one window operation solution for a complete event.
  2. Decore: Our dedicated team works closely with clients to define their unique vision, from creating detailed design plans and storyboards.
  3. Fine Dining Catering: Your wedding is one of the most imprtant day of your life. A celebration to be cherished forever.
  4. Venue Service: Marcopolo Events provides their best service at various Venue Options available on our panel.
  5. Corporate Catering: Getting quality and healthy meals for your lunch is often a challenge. We are committed to get this sorted.
  6. Wedding Planner: Our background of providing quality services dates back from our 60 year old company "Haji Tent House".
