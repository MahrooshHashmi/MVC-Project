About Mintography

Mintography is a production wing of Mintbrains (Private) Limited responsible to look after clients request for animation, videography, photography, aerial photography, aerial videography or short videos, used for promos or as a digital branded content. 

Features

Our wide range of services are not only related to merely wedding photography as is the general perception of photography companies. In order to differentiate from the ret we have a number of services that we offer such as

Wedding Photography & video

Corporate branding

Animated videos

Slideshow presentations

Outdoor shoots (including models)

Drone experiences

Time lapse

Hyper/Motion Lapse

Live broadcast (Facebook Live etc.)

Testimonial videos

And many more!
